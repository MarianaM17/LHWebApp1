Privacy Policy

The 'List Hub Free' app DOES NOT transfer the data you enter into the lists you create or any user or device data to anyone at any time.

The 'List Hub Free' app DOES NOT have the capabilities to access your personal information such as: account info, contacts, location, calendar, email, microphone, music library, pictures library, video library, WiFi, etc...

The 'List Hub Free' app has the capability to access the webcam's video feed to capture snapshots from connected webcams, can access the files on removable storage, and can access your Internet connection.

The information you enter into the lists you create is stored into a local SQLite database, a third party public domain software. The SQLite database is created on the device you installed this application. You can find the SQLite privacy policy at http://sqlite.org

Windows can include the lists data in automatic backups to OneDrive. This data may be included when you choose to have Windows make automated backups to OneDrive.

According to the Windows Store's APP DEVELOPER AGREEMENT at

https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/windows/apps/hh694058.aspx

"This application uses the Microsoft Error Reporting Data process solely for the purpose of testing and resolving compatibility issues, fixing and resolving “bugs” or other quality issues. Error Reporting Data may include Personal Information."
